Clustering

It is combining locations of close proximity into clusters, and simplify the display of markers on the map. There a number on a cluster indicates how many locations it contains. You can click on each cluster and map zooms into that location and cluster splits into smaller clusters or individual location. You can also zoom into any of the cluster locations, the number on the cluster decreases, and you begin to see the individual location on the map. Zooming out of the map consolidates the locations into clusters again. When you click on an individual location a popup will apper with related info of that perticular location. Info will contain the properties sent in the file.

Reset button

Notice that there is a reset button on the top. If you click on that button to reset the map into default cordinates and default zoom
